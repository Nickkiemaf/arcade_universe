Arcade Game

A multi-game platform for children 4-12 years old.

The Arcade gives players access to two different games.

  • Choices

    Children can choose to play between Rock, Paper, Scissors or Guess My Numberr games to rank higher in the Arcade Universe.

  • PG Ratings

    The Arcade is a safe haven for children. The universe incorporates games suitable for children aged 4-12 years. Games can be played with zero adult supervision.

  • Fun

    Unlimited fun experience in the Arcade Universe.

The Arcade Game was inspired by the core desire to incoprate fun games into a platform for children. My little cousins loves to play games but the internet is saturated with violent and zero-value added entertainments. The desire to ensure they have maximum fun while playing is what inspired this game.
